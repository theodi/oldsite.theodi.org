2. TERM

2.1. This Agreement shall commence on the Commencement Date and, unless otherwise terminated in accordance with the terms of the Agreement, shall continue until the later of the Expiry Date or the completion of the Services.


3. PERFORMANCE OF SERVICES

3.1. During the Term, the ODI shall provide to the Client the Services and shall provide the Personnel to perform the Services.

3.2. The ODI will perform the Services using reasonable skill and care.

3.3. The Client will use reasonable endeavours to provide to the ODI in good time all information, approvals, assistance and resources needed to enable the ODI to perform the Services in accordance with this Agreement and the ODI is entitled to rely upon the same.

3.4. The ODI will not be required to provide the Services in the case of illness or accident to the Personnel.

3.5. The ODI may, with the prior written agreement of the Client, provide a substitute to the Personnel to perform the Services. In such event, the daily rate of the substitute will be the same as the Personnel that has been replaced.

3.6. The ODI will endeavour to complete the Services by the Expiry Date using the allocated number of days as set out in the Statement of Works. If for any reason the ODI is unable to complete the Services by the Expiry Date or within the allocated number of days, the ODI will notify the Client and be entitled to an extension of time. The fees payable as a result of any Services being performed over a longer period of time than set out in the Statement of Works will be calculated on the daily rate of the Personnel providing the Services.

3.7. The ODI will take steps it deems appropriate to remedy any defect for which it is responsible for causing in any of the Deliverables provided that such defects are notified to the ODI in writing within 30 days of the Client’s receipt of the Deliverables.


4. FEES AND EXPENSES

4.1. Subject to any Special Condition set out in the Statement of Works, on signing of the engagement letter/contract, the ODI shall render an invoice to the Client in respect of 50% of the total Fees/contract value. The Client shall pay this amount within 14 days of the date of the invoice received from the ODI.

4.2. On completion of the Services, the ODI shall render an invoice to the Client in respect of the balance of the total Fees/contract value plus any reasonable expenses incurred whilst performing the Services. The Client shall pay this amount within 14 days of the date of the invoice received from the ODI.

4.3. Fees are quoted exclusive of VAT and the ODI will add VAT to its invoices where appropriate.

4.4. The daily and hourly rates of the Personnel are subject to review on the 1 January of each year and the Client agrees the Fees payable by the Client to the ODI may be adjusted to account for such review.

4.5. Without prejudice to the ODI’s other rights and remedies, the ODI will be entitled to interest on any overdue amounts due to the ODI at the statutory rate of interest in accordance with the Late Payment of Commercial Debts (Interests) Act 1998. Such interest shall accrue on a daily basis from the due date until the actual date of payment which the Client shall pay together with the overdue amount.
