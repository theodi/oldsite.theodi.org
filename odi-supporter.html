Membership for organisations

Join the leading global open data network and explore the business benefits of open data.

Being an ODI Supporter has led directly to a revenue generating partnership of £50,000-£100,000,” - Sarah Hitchcock, GeoLytix

  1. Connect and collaborate
  2. Explore the benefits of open data
  3. Unlock new business opportunities

By becoming an ODI Supporter, you will be able to:

1. Connect and collaborate

  • Receive an exclusive discounted ticket to the ODI’s annual summit conference
  • Receive free tickets to quarterly ODI Connect member networking events
  • Hear first about other ODI events
  • Meet our network of members, startups and international nodes
  • Hear about tenders and collaboration opportunities

2. Explore the benefits of open data

  • Save 20% on ODI public training courses
  • Get the latest insights, research and developments from members and the ODI with our monthly newsletter
  • Join the conversation with other members on Twitter and our members-only LinkedIn group
  • Get the latest open data research and news to stay ahead of competitors

3. Unlock new business opportunities

  • Showcase your open data work - contribute to blog posts and case studies, demonstrate your products at ODI Connect and more
  • Access potential collaboration and research opportunities
  • Be listed in the ODI member directory and annual report
  • Show your organisation’s commitment to open data - display an ODI Supporter badge
  • Affiliate your brand with our world-class team, setting open data best-practice
