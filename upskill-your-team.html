Upskill your team

Investing in the right kind of learning for your team can be transformative. The ODI has a broad spectrum of training and learning options to suit all organisations, big and small.

Our work is underpinned by research and insights from clients in businesses and governments around the world. The ODI was co-founded in 2012 by the inventor of the web Sir Tim Berners-Lee and AI expert Sir Nigel Shadbolt to address today’s global challenges using the web of data.

Publish and use data more effectively

Investing in the right kind of learning for your team can be transformative. The ODI has a broad spectrum of training and learning options to suit all organisations, big and small.

The Local Government Association (LGA) wanted to improve how local authorities published and used data.The ODI helped to upskill hundreds of council workers across England with blended learning tailored to their needs. Six bespoke eLearning modules are helping users learn about standards, open data publishing techniques and how to use their data to better effect, and a series of private webinars helped them to apply their knowledge effectively.

We recognise the ODI as the leading experts in the field of open data. Through working with the ODI, we are ensuring we maintain best practice in data publishing in local government.

— Dr Tim Adams, Programme Manager, Research and Information, Local Government Association
