Open Data Challenge Series - Programme Lead

The Open Data Immersion Programme is looking to create compelling stories and new businesses from the use of Open Data. We are keen to collaborate with passionate and driven members of the open data community to work with Government and Industry to make that happen.

We are inviting people/organisations to apply to lead on each series. The successful applicant will have experience of running open data events and initiatives, such as hack weekends, as well as an active interest in the sector and knowledge which would enable them to convene thought leaders, industry experts and activists.

Format of each programme

Each programmewill span over a nine month period and constitute:

  • Recruitment of a ProgrammeLead -recruitment andappointment of a domain expert to ensure the successful and relevant delivery of the programme
  • Challenge definition day-convening data owners, data users and data consumers, thought leaders and industry experts to explore and shape the challenges for the series
  • Applications open-the formal launch of the competition when applications for participation happen
  • Development/Acceleration period-an opportunity for teams to form, ideas to consolidate and develop, data to be reviewed, improved and released
  • Creation and Innovation Weekend-where the products and tools are developed, tested and presented to a panel of judges. Seed funding is awarded to three teams
  • Incubation,mentoring and review-further development of the three funded ideas including access to relevant expertise;judging undertaken by Nesta and winner selected

Requirements of the ProgrammeLead

We want to partner with people who will help us to deliver inspirational events which help us to enable change, unlock the supply of open data and stimulate the demand. The ODI will rely on you to bring together the right blend of people to participate in the sector series, including:

  • sector thought leaders
  • an industry sponsor
  • domain experts
  • host venues

You will liaise closely with colleagues in the ODI and Nestato ensure that the objectives of the event are met but will have the freedom to involve other parties as required (e.g. facilitators, events management). You will work closely with our partners on the management of the prizes and impact assessment.

Experience Required

We are looking for people who know the sector landscape, the open data community and have experience of arranging events such as hacks, competitions and workshops. You will have a collaborative approach and be passionate about delivering tangible outputs that will progress the open data agenda.

Compensation

The Programme Lead will work together with The ODI and Nesta to deliver the programme over a nine month period. The three parties are responsible for a budget of c. £70k, approximately £20k of which is set aside to cover time committed by the Programme Lead.
