Competition: help us rename ODMAT

This competition has now closed.

The Open Data Maturity Model project is offering you a chance to name the new online assessment tool being created in partnership with Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs (Defra) for use by all.

Funded by the Release of Data Fund, the ODI and Defra is producing a simple tool to enable organisations to assess their effectiveness at publishing and consuming open data. Developed from the Open Data Maturity Model, the tool will provide a simple scorecard and recommended areas of improvement.

To enter, simply [email your suggestion](mailto:[email protected]?subject=ODMAT naming competition) to [[email protected]](mailto:[email protected]?subject=ODMAT naming competition). The winning name will be selected by a panel of the Open Data Maturity Model project team.

The competition winner will be announced at the end of March before launching the online tool.

So what’s in it for me?

In addition to naming the tool that will be widely used by open data practitioners across the UK, the winner will receive a £50 gift voucher for a retailer of their choice.

Entry details

  • Entries will be accepted from 00:01 on Monday, 9 March 2015, to 18:00 on Friday, 13 March 2015.
  • Email your suggestion to [[email protected]](mailto:[email protected]?subject=ODMAT naming competition) with your full name and contact number.
  • The winning name will be selected by our panel of judges consisting of representatives from both Defra and the ODI.
  • Due to the anticipated volume of entries received, only the successful entrant will be informed ahead of any announcement within the public domain.
  • The competition winner will be announced on the ODI and Defra websites during the week commencing 16 March 2015. More details will be announced soon.
