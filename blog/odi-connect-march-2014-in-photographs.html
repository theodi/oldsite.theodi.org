ODI Connect in photographs

2014-03-31 by The ODI


On March 26th we held the third ODI Connect networking evening at HM Treasury. The event was themed around Government procurement.

We heard from Liam Maxwell, CTO, HM Government, and a panel of experts on topics ranging from SME engagement in government, G-Cloud and the Digital Services Framework.

Over one hundred ODI members, civil servants and startups were in attendance. For the first time we featured a Demo Cafe, where some of our members could showcase their work, creating discussion and interest.
