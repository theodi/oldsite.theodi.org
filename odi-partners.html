ODI Partners

Getting ahead with open data

ODI Partners get access to unrivalled brand benefits, innovation and connectivity through the world's foremost and most agile open data organisation and through our global network. Whether your business is health, finance, telecoms, retail, transport, construction, engineering, education, data, charity or any other sector, you will join a group of distinguished peers and reap the benefits of open data engagement with specialist consultancy and invaluable exposure.

Partnerships start from £70,000 per annum
(in-kind commitments can make up part of the subscription at the ODI’s discretion).

Contact us to find out more.

Executive innovation workshop

Engage your senior team with data experts to consider how to unlock and establish the tangible value of open data to your business. Bring up to eight senior people to a half-day workshop with specialist trainers, technical advisers and contributions from leading thinkers and practitioners.

Strategic open data consultation

Explore a new way of thinking and working. We assist you to accelerate open data use cases and enable your team and organisation to capitalise on your opportunity.

Complimentary Annual Summit and Gala Dinner tickets

Worth up to £1000 each, Partners receive three Annual Summit conference places and three Gala Dinner places. This extremely high-profile event, limited to approx 350 places, attracts global open data experts, government and businesses leaders, and offers a unique opportunity to network with those innovators, practitioners and leaders shaping the open data landscape in industry, government, academia and the global community.

ODI Connect networking events

Partners receive four tickets to each of our biannual member networking events and an extra ‘guest’ ticket to introduce another company. ODI Connect convenes government, industry and academia with startups, innovators and SMEs. ODI’s team strive to help make you meaningful connections and elevate your organisation’s offerings and visual presence.

Training discounts

20% saving on a range of public courses. We will assist you to build capacity, skills and new expertise. ODI trained more than 240 people from 11 countries in our first year.

Brand and marketing value

Showcase your work in the ODI member directory. Create an ODI guest blog with extensive social media exposure. Feature in ODI case studies, marketing and the ODI annual report and affiliate your brand with open data best practice. The more you're involved, the greater the exposure, marketing and brand value you'll receive.

Strength in certification

Learn best practice through access to tools for using and publishing with Open Data Certificates and boost your data's credibility by being nurtured and shared with experts.

Stay in the loop

Access technical and business research, receive regular updates on open data events, and receive a monthly member newsletter.

Space to meet, work and grow

Members can access the ODI collaborative space designed to facilitate networking. The ODI's international community of members opens up a huge variety of potential collaborations and new business, with startups and the node network, reaching new territories.

We also have stand alone sponsorship opportunities starting from £30,000.
