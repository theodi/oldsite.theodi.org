Training team

ODI Training Programme Leaders

ODI Programme Leaders are fully-qualified trainers who hold a professional qualification or equivalent record in high quality training.

ODI Registered Trainers

'ODI Registered Trainer' is a quality mark for trainers who have been assessed by ODI Programme Leads as able to train others in open data. They can deliver high quality paid-for open data training under the ODI Registered Trainer badge.

If you think you have what it takes to be an open data trainer, our Train the Trainer course might be for you. Have a look at the assessment criteria and fill in the application form to register your interest.

If you would like an ODI Registered Trainer to deliver some training, please get in contact via [email protected].
